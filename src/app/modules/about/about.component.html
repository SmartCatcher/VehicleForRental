Vadagaivandi enables you to book a vehicle in a tap and provides accurate distance and rate calculation. In just a few minutes, you can hire a truck through the call center, app or website from the comfort of home or office. Vadagaivandi is a tech based logistics platform which provides simplified and cost effective last mile connectivity to customers as well as higher earning potential for truck drivers.
